Profile of Ning University

Ningbo University was founded in 1986 with a donation from Mr. Bao Yugang, the World Ship King. Comrade Deng Xiaoping wrote the name of the university, and it is a national "Double First Class" construction university. It is a university jointly built by Zhejiang Province, the Ministry of Education, and Ningbo City, as well as a university jointly built by the State Oceanic Administration and Ningbo City. It is one of the first key construction universities in Zhejiang Province.

In the process of its establishment and development, the school has received care, support, and assistance from party committees and governments at all levels, the Ningbo Gang at home and abroad, and various sectors of society. At the beginning of its establishment, Peking University, Fudan University, Zhejiang University, University of Science and Technology of China, and the former Hangzhou University provided assistance to build the school, starting from a high starting point; In 1992, it was included in the first batch of colleges and universities nationwide for enrollment; In 1995, it passed the first batch of qualified evaluation for undergraduate teaching work by the former National Education Commission; In 1996, the former Ningbo University, Ningbo Normal University, and Zhejiang Fisheries University Ningbo Branch merged to form the new Ningbo University; In 2000, it was listed as a key construction university by the Zhejiang Provincial Government; In 2011, it became a co built university between the State Oceanic Administration and Ningbo City; In 2012, it became a jointly built university by Zhejiang Province, the Ministry of Education, and Ningbo City; Selected as one of the first key construction universities in Zhejiang Province in 2015; In 2017, it was selected as a national "Double First Class" construction university.

After several generations of hard work and entrepreneurship by Mr. Ning, the school has become a comprehensive teaching and research oriented university, with comprehensive strength firmly ranking among the top 100 universities in China. We have 12 disciplines including economics, law, education, literature, history, science, engineering, agriculture, medicine, management, art, and interdisciplinary fields. We have 23 colleges, 10 directly affiliated research institutions, and 5 directly affiliated hospitals. According to data released by ESI, 12 disciplines including engineering, clinical medicine, chemistry, materials science, animal and plant science, agricultural science, environment and ecology, pharmacology and toxicology, biology and biochemistry, computer science, physics, and general social science have entered the top 1% of world academic institutions. There are currently 9 doctoral degree authorization points in first level disciplines, 9 postdoctoral research mobile stations, 31 master's degree authorization points in first level disciplines, 27 master's degree authorization categories, and 59 undergraduate enrollment majors. Having the right to recommend master's degree students without examination, being eligible to accept Chinese government scholarships and international Chinese teacher scholarships for international students coming to China, and being eligible to accept students from Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan. There are currently 20162 full-time undergraduate students, 12412 graduate students of various types (including 213 international students), 1126 international students (including 986 academic students), and 7287 higher education continuing education students. There are currently 3157 faculty members (including 2149 teaching and research personnel), including 525 senior professional and technical personnel, 698 deputy senior professional and technical personnel, and 1711 doctoral degree holders. The main campus is located in the north area of Ningbo Higher Education Park, and there is also the Meishan campus, with a total area of 3055 acres. The total area of the library building is 51000 square meters, with 3.2396 million paper books and 2.584 million electronic books. The total value of instruments and equipment is 1.31 billion yuan.
